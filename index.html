...a bit about me..

A long time ago, in a galaxy known to many as "The Apple," my journey began. It's been a swerving path through many locales, years of drum & bugle corps tours (& endless bus breakdowns), writing music for young people to play in competitions, playing the drums & bass in a variety of different settings--for dancing or for just listening...and lately? 2 years of non-stop coding & learning web technologies, to build useful 21st-Century tools for people.

Through it all, my passion has been getting things right--whether it be crafting a marching band arrangement that makes you forget you're even listening to a marching band, or kicking a big band and keeping it in a solid pocket--or constructing clean, logical code that does what it's supposed to do, as efficiently as possible. Going the extra mile, keeping an eye on the big picture, and making sure the details are covered as well as can be--that's my paradigm.
